A Hanimex Vista Viewer II — a 1970s film slide viewer — repurposed as a passive information display. A Raspberry Pi Zero sits inside, querying NASA, iNaturalist, and a handful of other public APIs to pull down images and data. No interaction required. It just sits there and tells you things about the world.

Built because I wanted something on my desk that wasn't a screen I had to manage.
