A one-man electronic music project built entirely on hardware — vintage synthesizers, drum machines, a Behringer TD-3. No DAW, no laptop on stage, no plugins. Everything happens in real time on the machines.

The name is accurate. The music is for the end times but it's still pretty fun to dance to.
